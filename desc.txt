International Business Machines Corporation (IBM) is a global technology and consulting company headquartered in Armonk, New York. Founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR) and renamed IBM in 1924, it is a multinational corporation that operates in over 170 countries. IBM is known for its wide range of technology and consulting services, including cloud computing, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and hardware such as mainframe computers and servers. IBM has a rich history of innovation, contributing to advancements in computing, such as the development of the personal computer, the relational database, and the programming language Fortran. The company also invests heavily in research and development, maintaining one of the largest industrial research organizations in the world.
